Datacenter Potential Across the United States

This prototype explores where future datacenters might be located in the U.S. by visualizing a placeholder "datacenter potential" score for each state. Future iterations will combine electricity demand data from the EIA with additional infrastructure, environmental, and economic factors.

Interactive Map of Datacenter Potential

How to Use This Prototype

Use the dropdown above to change which metric is used to color each state. Hover over a state to see its name and placeholder scores. In the final project, these scores will be derived from real data including hourly electricity demand (EIA), renewable generation, climate, and other infrastructure constraints.

Next Steps
